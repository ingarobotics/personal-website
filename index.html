I am based in San Francisco, and I am currently interested in physical-world constraints and manufacturing.

In college, I've researched multimodal invasive BCIs for text, speech, and avatar decoding at UCSF Chang Lab and Berkeley Speech Group. Such neuroprosthesis enabled a pathway to restoring naturalistic communication for people with severe paralysis, and we've published this work in Nature.
